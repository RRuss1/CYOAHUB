# CYOAhub -- FAQ

---

## Getting Started

**What is CYOAhub?**
A multiplayer AI-powered RPG platform. You and your friends play through a collaborative story driven by an AI Game Master -- making choices, fighting enemies, and exploring worlds together. No downloads, no accounts, just open and play.

**Is it free?**
Yes. No account, no signup, no paywall.

**Do I need to know how to play D&D or any tabletop RPG?**
No. The AI GM explains everything as you go. If you can read and pick a choice, you can play.

**What devices does it work on?**
Any modern browser -- desktop, tablet, or phone. No app download required.

**Does it work offline?**
No. The AI Game Master requires an internet connection.

## Worlds

**What worlds can I play in?**

Three official worlds ship with CYOAhub:

- **Stormlight Chronicles** -- Epic fantasy set in Brandon Sanderson's Cosmere. 10 Radiant Orders, Surgebinding magic, Shardblades, a 5-Oath progression system.
- **D&D 5e** -- Classic fantasy using the Official Basic Rules. Fighter, Cleric, Rogue, Wizard. Humans, Dwarves, Elves, Halflings. Spells and monsters.
- **The Wretched Deep** -- A dark, paranoid survival RPG. 5 archetypes, Obsession/Corruption mechanics, the Hollow Crown whispering in the dark.

You can also play in **community-built worlds** published by other players, or build your own.

**How do I build my own world?**
Use the World Builder wizard (click "Create a World" from the landing page). It walks you through 7 steps:

1. **Identity** -- Name, description, era, tone, technology level
2. **Magic** -- Whether magic exists, what it's called, what fuels it, how risky it is
3. **Stats** -- Pick from 16 stat systems (classic D&D six-stat, 3-stat simple, cyberpunk, emotional, survival horror, and more)
4. **World Facts** -- Factions, locations, central conflict, lore, naming conventions
5. **Atmosphere** -- Ambient soundscape and enemy categories to encounter
6. **GM Personality** -- Narrator style, combat frequency, lethality, NPC depth, world physics, death rules
7. **Visual Identity** -- Colors, fonts, UI style, card art

Your world becomes fully playable immediately -- the AI GM adapts to your lore, tone, and rules.

**Can I share my world?**
Yes. At the end of the wizard you can publish it to the Community Hub. Anyone can browse and start a campaign in it.

**Can I delete my world?**
Yes. A delete button appears on worlds you created. Other players cannot delete your worlds.

## Gameplay

**How does the AI Game Master work?**
After each player action, the AI generates a story beat -- describing consequences, raising stakes, and presenting 4 new choices. It adapts to your decisions, remembers what happened, and keeps the narrative consistent. Each world has its own GM personality, lore, and tone.

**How many players can play together?**
2 to 5 per campaign (party size is set when you create the campaign). Empty slots can be filled with AI companions.

**Can I play solo?**
Yes. Create a campaign, and the AI fills companion slots. Works the same as multiplayer -- the GM just runs their turns for you.

**How long is a campaign?**
A full campaign runs 180 turns across 3 acts. Sessions are async -- you don't all need to be online at the same time. Play a few turns, close the tab, come back later.

**What if a player disconnects?**
Game state saves after every action. Anyone can resume where things left off. The host can skip absent players' turns.

**How does combat work?**
When the story escalates into a fight, the game switches to a dedicated combat screen. Each player picks an action (attack, defend, heal, or use abilities), then the round resolves simultaneously -- dice rolls, damage, conditions, injuries. Enemy types are drawn from the world's configured enemy pool.

**What are the enemy categories?**
24 categories spanning fantasy and sci-fi:

*Fantasy:* Undead, Demons, Dragons, Giants, Goblinoids, Beasts, Fey, Elementals, Aberrations, Sea Creatures, Lycanthropes, Plants, Human Enemies, Mythological, Swarms

*Sci-Fi:* Robots, Cyborgs, Drones, Mechs, Androids, Aliens, Post-Apocalyptic, Corporate/PMC, Steampunk

World builders choose which categories are active. The AI GM spawns contextually appropriate enemies from those pools during combat.

**What does progression look like?**
Depends on the world:
- *Stormlight* -- Speak Oaths to advance through 5 Ideals, unlocking Surgebinding abilities
- *D&D 5e* -- Level-based progression with ability score increases
- *Wretched Deep* -- Corruption stages -- gain power but risk losing your character
- *Custom worlds* -- Whatever the world builder configured (oaths, levels, milestones, or freeform)

## Characters

**How do I create a character?**
When you join a campaign, a short creation flow walks you through: pick a class or background path, allocate stats, choose ancestry, select equipment, and optionally bond a companion. Takes about 2 minutes.

**What options are there?**
Each world defines its own:
- **Stormlight** -- 10 Radiant Orders or 6 Hero Paths. Human or Singer ancestry.
- **D&D 5e** -- 4 classes, 4 races, 6 backgrounds. Spells for casters.
- **Wretched Deep** -- 5 archetypes (Lurker, Thief, Watcher, Whisperer, Fleshwright) or 5 Past Lives. 4 ancestries.
- **Custom worlds** -- 4 default archetypes with world-specific flavor, or whatever the creator configured.

**Do characters carry over between campaigns?**
No. Each campaign starts fresh. Your story and choices are saved permanently as a chronicle.

**Can my character die?**
Depends on the world. Some use permanent death, some allow revival, some treat it as a narrative event. The world creator sets this.

## Audio

**What's the ambient audio?**
CYOAhub generates atmospheric soundscapes in real time using your browser's audio engine -- no files to download. 21 soundscapes available:

Storm, Forge, Ocean, Forest, Dungeon, Tavern, Desert, Arctic, Jungle, Volcanic, Cathedral, Battlefield, Cosmic, Swamp, Clockwork, Neon City, Wasteland, Spaceship, Deep Sea, Ruins, Market

Each world defaults to a soundscape that fits its setting. You can toggle audio on/off and adjust volume during play.

## Data & Privacy

**How is my game saved?**
Campaign state saves to the cloud after every action. You can close the browser and come back -- nothing is lost.

**What's stored in my browser vs. the cloud?**
- *Browser (local storage):* Which campaigns/worlds you created (ownership), your last character, preferences
- *Cloud:* Campaign game state, game log, published worlds

**What happens if I clear my browser data?**
Your games still exist in the cloud. You just lose ownership markers -- meaning the delete button disappears for your campaigns and worlds. The data itself is safe.

**Is my data private?**
Yes. Campaign content is not shared publicly. Only worlds you explicitly publish appear in the Community Hub.

## Troubleshooting

**The game isn't loading / I see a blank screen.**
Try a hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R). CYOAhub requires JavaScript enabled and a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari).

**The AI GM isn't responding.**
The AI requires an internet connection and the proxy server to be running. If responses are slow, the AI service may be under heavy load -- wait a moment and try again.

**My character didn't save.**
Character creation saves when you hit the final "Create" button and reach the lobby screen. If you closed the browser mid-creation, you'll need to create again.

**Audio isn't playing.**
Browsers block audio until you interact with the page. Click the audio toggle button to start. If still silent, check that your device volume is up and no other tab is blocking audio.

**I created a world but it's gone.**
Private worlds are stored in your browser's local storage. If you cleared browser data or switched devices, private worlds won't appear. Published worlds are in the cloud and will still show in the Community Hub.

## Community & Contributing

**How do I report a bug or give feedback?**
Use the Feedback option in the hamburger menu (top-right corner). It links to our GitHub Issues page.

**Can I contribute to CYOAhub?**
Yes. CYOAhub is open source. Visit the GitHub repo through the Contribute option in the menu. You can build new worlds, design enemy patterns, improve the AI prompts, or work on the UI.

**Can my community world become an Official world?**
Worlds that demonstrate quality world-building and get strong engagement from players may be promoted to Official status.
